Icon-only square control for toolbars, card corners, and navigation. Always pass `aria-label`.

```jsx
<IconButton aria-label="Save to favourites" variant="soft"><HeartIcon/></IconButton>
<IconButton aria-label="More" variant="ghost">⋯</IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost`, `soft`, `outline`, `solid` (Pine). Sizes `sm(32) | md(40) | lg(48)`. Keep the glyph a stroked icon (1.5px) for consistency.
