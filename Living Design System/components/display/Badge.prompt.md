Small status pill with semantic tones. Use for statuses like Available, Pending, Sold.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Available</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning">Pending</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand" variant="solid">New</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | brand | accent | success | warning | danger | info`. Variants `soft | solid | outline`. Keep labels one or two words.
